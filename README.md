🛡️ Trustforge AI

Verify before you trust. Empowering users through transparent AI security. Trustforge is a powerful AI-driven security and verification hub designed to protect users from modern digital threats. It goes beyond traditional “black-box” scanners by providing clear explanations, confidence scores, 
and educational insights—helping users not only detect threats but understand them. From phishing emails to fake news and malicious websites, Trustforge ensures you stay informed, aware, and secure.

✨ Key Features
⚡ Multi-Source Threat Detection
Analyze emails, URLs, social media posts, and articles—all in one place.
🤖 AI-Powered Analysis
Uses advanced AI models to classify content as Safe ✅, Suspicious ⚠️, or Malicious 🚨.
📊 Confidence Score
Get a percentage-based certainty level for every analysis.
🔍 Explainable Results
No black boxes—see the top reasons why something is flagged.
🚨 Actionable Security Links
Direct access to the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal of India when threats are detected.
📄 Report Generation
Export detailed scan reports in PDF format for documentation.
🎓 User Education Focus
Learn how threats work so you can identify them in the future.
